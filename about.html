My Little Story

01.

My photography journey started all the way back in middle school, taking whatever photos I could with my cheap phone camera, some of them came out pretty decent (at least thats what I thought at the time). I started to enjoy taking pictures more and more, but I never bought a professional camera or even thought about making this a profession until I was 20 years old. I bought my first camera and soon after I started doing professional shoots and charging for work that I'm very proud of.

From nature photography to portrait photography, I love all aspects of photography and the many styles that can be done. I plan to hone my skills in each of these different ways of shooting one photoshoot at a time!
