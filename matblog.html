Matthew's Photoshoot

  • 14 june 2020
  • -
  • -
  • Jaden Scott

Matthew was one of my more recent shoots, he wanted to do something quick before he left from Charlotte. He posted a gig on Craigslist and my portfolio caught his eye the most out of everyone he viewed. I was excited to be the one to do this for him, and I started to finally feel like I could make this a life long career.

Like Olivias photoshoot, we went around to anywhere in Charlotte that we could find to get the best photos possible. I tried to get the most focus on his eyes, as you can see in most of the photos they pop out a lot with the different locations, especially in the close ups.

Jaden Scott
